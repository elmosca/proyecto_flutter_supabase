# Quick Usage Guide (Students)

## Main menu (top bar and hamburger)
- Navigate between Anteprojects, Projects, Tasks and Kanban Board.
- Use the language icon to switch Spanish/English.
- The bell icon shows notifications.

## Basic flow
1. Create or review your Anteproject from Anteprojects.
2. Once Approved, it will appear as a Project.
3. In Projects you will see tabs: Details, Comments, Files, Kanban and Tasks.
4. Use Kanban to organize work and Tasks to track progress.

## Tips
- Keep your tasks up to date (status and dates).
- Check notifications frequently.
- If unsure, ask your tutor using the Comments section of the anteproject/project.
